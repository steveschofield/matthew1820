Matthew 18:20 Project - Fine Print

Matt18:20 "For where two or three have gathered together in My name, I am there in their midst.” (NASB)

There is fine print! And it is awesome non-legal type. All prayers are encrypted and not shared except between you and God. The Matthew 18:20 project was God inspired to follow Matthew 6:6. The Matthew 18:20 project tracks "with permission" location & "prayer word count". Prayer warriors can subscribe to receive notifications with number of words and total number of prayers from each day.
